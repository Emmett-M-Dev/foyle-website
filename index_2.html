01 — Our story

A family-run school in the heart of Ireland.

Founded in 1990 by Director Paul Murray, Foyle International has spent more than three decades helping students from around the world improve their English and broaden their horizons.

Foyle International is a private English language school specialising in educational tourism. We deliver a complete experience — language classes, local accommodation, cultural activities and weekend excursions, all wrapped in the famously warm Irish welcome that brings students back year after year.

As the longest-established language school in Northern Ireland, and the first ever to receive the prestigious British Council Accreditation, we hold ourselves to the highest standards in everything we do. Our friendly staff and experienced teachers make sure every student's time in Derry is time well spent.

We attract students from all over the world — and it's no accident that so many come back. Derry has a way of staying with you.

Paul Murray Director & Founder · Est. 1990

03 — Your home in Ireland

About Derry.

Set on the banks of the River Foyle, Derry/Londonderry is one of the most welcoming cities in Ireland — and one of the best places in the world to learn English.

"Many of our former students return year after year because they know they're guaranteed the highest quality education, a warm Irish welcome, and a unique cultural experience they'll never forget."

Derry is Ireland's only completely intact walled city — safe, walkable, and alive with culture, history and music. It's the perfect place to practise English outside the classroom, make friends from across Europe, and discover a part of Ireland that most tourists miss.

Our students improve their English through real-life immersion: cafés, city walks, local events, and the kind of conversations you can only have in a small, friendly city.
