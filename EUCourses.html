About the programme

The EU's programme for education & mobility.

Erasmus+ is the EU's programme to support education, training, youth and sport in Europe, with an estimated budget of €26.2 billion. Foyle participates in both Key Action 1 and Key Action 2 programmes.

The 2021–2027 programme places a strong focus on social inclusion, the green and digital transitions, and promoting young people's participation in democratic life.

We offer a range of work internships and placements across the island of Ireland. Professional work experience is a great way to add an international element to your CV and improve your level of English. Internships can be funded through Erasmus+ or funded privately (EU students only).

KA1 · Hosting staff mobilities

Erasmus+ Teacher Development Course.

The Erasmus+ Teacher Development Course is a flexible, tailored solution for English language teachers who want to refresh and update their teaching and language skills.

The course offers a stimulating blend of theory and practice, and will equip you with a wealth of practical ideas and resources to inject energy into your classes and enhance the teaching and learning experience for both you and your students.

Every school and teaching context is different — we will work with you to design a course that is relevant to your teaching needs, delivered in a motivating yet relaxed atmosphere.

Who is this course for?

  • Non-native teachers of English looking to develop their language and teaching skills
  • Teachers of teenagers, young adults and adults (12 years upwards)
  • Teachers with a strong intermediate level of English or above (B1+)
  • LevelB2 — Upper intermediate (B1+ welcome)
  • Class times9.30am–5pm · Monday–Friday
  • Hours30, 40 or 50 hours
  • Duration2 weeks
  • Class sizeMaximum 12 students
  • LocationDerry, Northern Ireland
How it works

Build your own course.

Each group decides how many hours of study they would like to do — 30, 40 or 50 hours. The next step is to choose your modules, combining the compulsory module with as many elective modules as you need.

Compulsory module · 15 hours

  1. Communicative Language Classes and Modern Teaching Methodologies

Elective modules · 5, 10 or 15 hours

  1. Modern Teaching Methodologies — Advanced
  2. English Language Development — Language and Skills Practice
  3. Teaching with Technology — Getting Started
  4. CLIL — Methodologies and Materials (Content & Language Integrated Learning)
  5. The Best of Both: British and Irish Culture
  6. Drama for Creative Language Learning
KA2 · Strategic partnerships

Our EU projects.

We have been at the forefront of improving quality in VET mobility for years — from Leonardo da Vinci partnerships through to Erasmus+ KA2 Strategic Partnerships, focusing on ECVET principles, VET practices, wellbeing, digital skills and youth.

Current KA2

Robots4Inclusion

Educational robotics to support inclusive education in primary schools — promoting inclusion, diversity and digital, informatic and technical skills. Six partners across Germany, Türkiye, Spain, Lithuania and Finland.

November 2023 – October 2026
Current KA2

TLN Mobilicat

A professional internship programme offering real work experience in another European country. Participants plan a personalised itinerary and build the skills that improve their access to the labour market.

July 2023 – July 2025
KA2

Tried & True (Essai Transformé)

Good practices of the after-Erasmus+ for learners and trainers — strengthening mentoring competencies and helping adult learners capitalise on the soft, vocational and intercultural skills gained during mobility.

February 2022 – February 2024
KA2

StayWell

An innovative web-learning approach for advancing and sustaining VET learners' wellbeing and mental health through social-emotional skills that help them study, master life and enter the job market.

November 2020 – October 2022
KA2

Against the Fake World

Tackling Fake News and Media Literacy among young people aged 14–18 — through research, student surveys and focus groups, stakeholder interviews and a final report of key findings across Europe.

January 2020 – December 2023
KA2

CORAL

Creating Opportunities for Adult Learners through entrepreneurial competencies — a pan-European pathway to up-skill and re-skill the workforce, supporting self-employment and progression to upper-secondary level.

September 2019 – September 2021
