02 — Why Derry

Not a tourist town. A real one.

Derry is intimate, walkable and genuinely warm — exactly what makes it the best place in the world to learn English.

The Ancient Walls

Walk the full circuit of Derry's 17th-century walls — one of the best-preserved in Europe and right in the heart of the city.

Halloween Capital of Europe

Every October, Derry transforms into Europe's biggest Halloween festival. If you're here in autumn, you won't forget it.

A Genuinely Warm City

Derry consistently ranks among the friendliest cities in the UK. Students don't just visit — they feel at home.

Food, Music & Culture

From the Walled City Brewery to live traditional music sessions, Derry has a real culture scene that surprises every visitor.

The Peace Bridge

An iconic landmark and symbol of the city's remarkable transformation — great for walks and one of the most photographed spots in Northern Ireland.

Safe & Walkable

The city centre is compact and safe. Students can walk from their host family to school, to the walls, to the river — all within minutes.
