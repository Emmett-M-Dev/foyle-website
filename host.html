What is homestay?

You live with a local Derry family — sharing meals, conversation and daily life. It's the fastest way to improve your English and the warmest introduction to Irish culture you'll find.

  • 24/7 Foyle support
  • Flexible stays – weekly or monthly
  • Payment every 2 weeks
